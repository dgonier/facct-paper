# FAccT 2026 Submission Guidelines

|-----------|------|

*All deadlines: 11:59 PM Anywhere On Earth*

## Formatting Requirements
- **Page limit:** 14 pages maximum (excluding references)
- **Ethics section:** Additional page allowed for ethics/adverse impacts
- **Revised submissions:** Up to 15 pages excluding references
- **Template:** ACM TAPS workflow (LaTeX or Word)
- **Anonymization:** Remove all author names, affiliations; cite own work in third person

## Submission Platform
- OpenReview: https://openreview.net/group?id=ACM.org/FAccT/2026/Conference
- Paper types: archival (full proceedings) or non-archival (abstract only)
- No concurrent submissions of substantially similar work

## Requirements
- At least one author must register, attend, and present
- ORCID ID required before acceptance
- Subject to ACM Publications Policies
